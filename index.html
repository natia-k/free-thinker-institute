Five Intentions

Better lives begin with better questions.

01

Devotion to Self Improvement

A personal devotion to self-improvement: physical, intellectual, emotional, and spiritual or philosophical.

02

Be Transparent & Open Minded

Recognize subjective truths, stay open to perspectives different from your own, and share your authentic self with others.

03

Critical Thinking

Improve judgment and rational thought rather than following blindly. Seek truth through reflection, reality, and experience.

04

Maximize Happiness, Minimize Harm

Pursue freedom, happiness, and progress while minimizing significant harm to yourself and others.

05

Give and Receive Love

Put more love into the world through support, generosity, respect, and real care for other human beings.

Goals

A community that is polite, prosperous, and full of good ideas.

The organization is still small, but growing. Its goals are ambitious and value-driven: create a warm, compassionate, open-minded community with an enriching and meaningful social life.

It also aims to help people become more prosperous, build valuable skills, and make enough money that money no longer determines their happiness.

Just as importantly, it wants to create and spread ideas that maximize happiness and minimize harm.
