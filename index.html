Five Intentions
Shared values for a better way of living together

1. Devotion to Self-Improvement

We value growth across physical, intellectual, emotional, and philosophical life, and we aim to become better versions of ourselves over time.

2. Transparency & Open-Mindedness

We try to stay honest about who we are, remain open to perspectives different from our own, and avoid closing ourselves off from new understanding.

3. Commitment to Critical Thinking

We encourage people to strengthen their judgment, think independently, and seek truth through reflection, evidence, and experience.

4. Maximize Happiness, Minimize Harm

We believe people should have as much freedom as possible to pursue fulfilling lives, while being thoughtful about harm to themselves and others.

5. Give & Receive Love

We want to contribute more care, compassion, and human warmth to the world, beginning with how we treat one another.

Goals
What this community hopes to build

Is Polite

We want to build a compassionate, welcoming community with a richer and more meaningful social life.

Is Prosperous

We want people to become financially secure enough that money stops being the central limit on wellbeing.

Shares Good Ideas

We want to create and spread ideas that lead to greater happiness and less harm in the world.

What We Do
Personal and professional development

Personal Development

Conversations explore values, growth, wellbeing, relationships, ideas, and other topics that matter to the group.

  • The core intentions and how they apply in everyday life
  • Personal and professional development topics
  • Other questions and interests brought by the community

Professional Development

Members support one another in identifying meaningful work, developing valuable skills, improving performance, and learning through others’ experience.

  • Clarifying your dream job direction
  • Building useful skillsets
  • Learning to excel in your current role
  • Connecting with experienced professionals

How participation works

Who can you interact with?

Prospective members, members, teachers, and leaders.

Where do people meet?

Through text chat, voice chat, video chat, and in-person gatherings.

Why participate?

To grow personally and professionally, and to contribute to a better world.

When can you participate?

Whenever you are free informally, and also through organized events.

How do you join?

By joining the Discord community and getting involved in conversations and events.

What do people discuss?

Career development, self-improvement, values, and ideas that help people live better together.
