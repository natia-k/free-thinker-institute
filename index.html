Who We Are

Community =
Unity + Diversity

We are building a warm, compassionate, open minded community centered on increasing happiness and minimizing harm.

The organization brings people together around personal and professional development, thoughtful discussion, and meaningful connection.

Everyone is welcome to join, learn, and help make these goals real.

The Six Intentions

Explore our shared framework for growth by selecting an icon.

✦

Reason & Fairness

Always use reason and intuition to seek truth and remain fair to others.

◌

Self-Discovery

Commit to personal growth across physical, emotional, and spiritual dimensions.

♡

Open-Mindedness

Treat others with respect and remain open to ideas that improve understanding.

○

Seeking Truth

Listen deeply and have the courage to consider that your beliefs might be wrong.

✳

Critical Thinking

Evaluate evidence with logic and distinguish fact from fiction.

♡

Love & Flourishing

Minimize harm and be eager to give, receive, and reciprocate love.

Minimize Harm, Maximize Flourishing & Love

Minimize significant unnecessary harm, try to maximize flourishing, and be eager to give, receive, and reciprocate love.
