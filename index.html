Our Pillars

The Free Thinker Institute prioritizes devotion to reason and intuition — using both to seek truth, act fairly, and grow with integrity.

We believe good decisions emerge from open minds willing to question, reconsider, and keep learning.

Our community values sincerity, integrity, wisdom, fairness, and meaningful connection.

Our Pillars
What We Stand For
01

Reason & Rational Thought

Teach people to trust and improve their own judgement and rational thought rather than following blindly. Seek truth about objective reality learnable through experimentation and the senses.

02

Freedom & Wellbeing

Have as much freedom as possible to pursue happiness while minimizing significant harm — for yourself and all others.

03

Love & Community

We all want to be loved, so we try to put more love into the world.

04

Critical Thinking

Commitment to critical thinking so we can better discern fact from fiction and wisdom from folly.

05

Transparency

Be transparent about who you are and what you believe, and actively consider opinions different from your own.

06

Self-Improvement

A personal devotion to growth — physical, intellectual, emotional, and philosophical.
