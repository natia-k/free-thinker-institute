Five Intentions

Better lives begin with better questions.

01

Self Improvement

A personal devotion to physical, intellectual, and emotional growth.

02

Transparency

Recognize subjective truths and share your authentic self with others.

03

Critical Thinking

Improve judgment and rational thought rather than following blindly.

04

Ethics

Pursue happiness while minimizing significant harm to others.

05

Compassion

Put more love into the world through support and real care.
