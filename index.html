The Five Intentions

01

Devotion to Self-Improvement

A commitment to growth—intellectual, physical, and emotional. We treat all individuals with respect.

02

Transparency & Open-Mindedness

Embrace everyone's subjective truths. Don't believe anything with 100% certainty; to do so closes the mind.

03

Critical Thinking

Trust your own judgment and rational thought rather than following blindly. Seek objective reality through experimentation.

04

Maximize Happiness, Minimize Harm

Pursue freedom while ensuring we minimize significant harm for ourselves and all sentient beings.

05

Giving & Receiving Love

We all want to be loved; we strive to put more love into the world for all sentient beings.
