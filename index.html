Who We Are

A community shaped by
reflection and growth.

We are a group of people trying to become wiser, more grounded, and more thoughtful in the way we live, relate, and work.

The Free Thinker Institute values reason, openness, honesty, self-improvement, and meaningful connection. We believe good lives are built through reflection, sincerity, and better decisions.

Our aim is to learn, question, grow, and support one another in creating more happiness and less harm where we can.

Five Intentions

A shared framework for
living and growing.

Five core commitments that shape the community’s spirit, conversations, and aspirations.

✦ 01

Devotion to Self Improvement

A commitment to growing physically, intellectually, emotionally, and philosophically over time.

◌ 02

Be Transparent & Open Minded

Stay honest about who you are, stay open to different viewpoints, and resist becoming fixed in your assumptions.

✳ 03

Committed to Critical Thinking

Use evidence, reflection, and clear judgment to better distinguish truth from confusion and noise.

○ 04

Maximize Happiness While Minimizing Harm

Seek freedom, fulfillment, and wellbeing while remaining mindful of harm to yourself and others.

♡ 05

Eager to Give and Receive Love

Create more warmth, compassion, affection, and meaningful connection in the lives around you.

How participation works.

Who can you interact with?

Members, prospective members, teachers, speakers, and community leaders.

Where do people meet?

In person, online through chat, and through voice or video conversation.

Why participate?

To grow personally, improve professionally, and contribute to a better culture.

When can you participate?

Through events, informal discussion, and whenever you have time to join in.

How do you join?
