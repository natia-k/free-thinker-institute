01 — Intentions

A shared framework for thinking and living.

These intentions shape how the community approaches growth, conversation, truth, and care. They should feel grounded, not preachy — more like principles than slogans.

01

Devotion to self-improvement

Physical, intellectual, emotional, and philosophical growth. A serious relationship to becoming more capable, more aware, and more honest with yourself.

02

Transparency and open-mindedness

Stay willing to revise your thinking. Consider other perspectives without collapsing into certainty too quickly. Truth is often found through attention, not rigidity.

03

Commitment to critical thinking

Learn to trust and refine your own judgment. Test ideas against evidence, experience, and reason rather than adopting them blindly.

04

Maximize happiness while minimizing harm

Seek a meaningful balance between freedom, well-being, responsibility, and care for others. A better life should not come at the cost of unnecessary harm.

05

Eager to give and receive love

A community is not only built on ideas, but also on warmth, presence, generosity, and the willingness to treat people with real humanity.

02 — Goals

A small organization with ambitious aims.

The point is not scale for its own sake. The point is to create a space that is actually useful: intellectually alive, socially warm, and materially supportive of people’s growth.

What the community is trying to build.

A place where people can become more thoughtful, more capable, and better connected to both their own values and other people. The work is personal and practical at once.

Goal 01
Create a warm, open-minded, respectful community with meaningful social life.
Goal 02
Help people become materially secure enough to pursue life with more freedom and dignity.
Goal 03
Develop and spread ideas that increase happiness and reduce unnecessary harm.
Community

Polite, open, and enriching

The tone matters. Intelligence without generosity becomes sterile. Growth without warmth becomes ego.

Prosperity

Support that extends into real life

Practical conversations around work, careers, skills, and direction — not only abstract ideals.

Ideas

Shared frameworks that actually help

Not content for content’s sake. Ideas that can be lived, tested, discussed, and improved.

03 — What We Do

Personal and professional development.

Conversations move between inner life and outer life: purpose, habits, work, ambition, community, and how to stay thoughtful while building something real.

Personal development

Conversations with depth.

The community discusses the five intentions, self-development, relationships, meaning, and whatever else feels worth thinking through carefully.

  • Reflection How to think more clearly about your life and choices.
  • Connection How to relate better, communicate better, and stay open.
  • Growth How to build discipline without losing humanity.
  • Meaning How values become practice, not just language.
Professional development

Dream job training.

The Institute also supports career direction and professional growth, helping members identify where they want to go and how to get better at the work.

  • Career direction Clarify the work you actually want to be doing.
  • Skill building Develop relevant strengths for the roles you aim toward.
  • Professional growth Learn how to improve in your current field.
  • Network Connect with experienced professionals and peers.
04 — Join

Who, where, and how to participate.

The structure stays simple. Join the Discord, attend conversations, participate when you can, and become part of a community built around thoughtfulness, openness, and practical growth.

Participation

A flexible format.

  • Who Prospective members, members, teachers, and leaders.
  • Where Text chat, voice, video, and in-person meetups in NYC / NJ.
  • When Informally whenever you’re free, plus scheduled events.
  • Why To improve personally and professionally, and help make life better for others too.
Training areas

Current areas of focus.

  • Product management Direction, strategy, communication, judgment.
  • Software development Technical growth and practical pathways.
  • User experience Research, design thinking, and product understanding.
  • QA / Sales and more Support across several professional directions.

Everyone is welcome.

Join the community through Discord, Zoom, or in person. Start with conversation. Stay for the clarity, growth, and sense of direction that comes from building better thought together.
