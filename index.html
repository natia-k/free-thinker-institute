Who We Are

Community =
Unity + Diversity

We are building a warm, compassionate, open minded community centered on increasing happiness and minimizing harm.

The organization brings people together around personal and professional development, thoughtful discussion, and meaningful connection.

Everyone is welcome to join, learn, and help make these goals real.

Six Intentions

A shared framework for
living and growing.

Six intentions shape how we think, relate, grow, seek truth, minimize harm, and build community together.

✦ 01

Reason, Intuition & Fairness

Hover / Tap
✦ 01

Reason, Intuition & Fairness

Always be polite while using reason and intuition to become the best version of yourself, seek truth, and be fair.

◌ 02

Self-Discovery & Self-Improvement

Hover / Tap
◌ 02

Self-Discovery & Self-Improvement

Commit to physical, intellectual, philosophical, emotional, and spiritual growth as a voluntary personal devotion.

♡ 03

Respect, Transparency & Open-Mindedness

Hover / Tap
♡ 03

Respect, Transparency & Open-Mindedness

Treat others with respect, be transparent about who you are, and stay open to new ideas that may improve your understanding.

○ 04

Truth & Deep Understanding

Hover / Tap
○ 04

Truth & Deep Understanding

Seek objective and subjective truths, listen deeply to others, and have the courage to consider that you may be wrong.

✳ 05

Commitment to Critical Thinking

Hover / Tap
✳ 05

Commitment to Critical Thinking

Gather evidence, evaluate it with logic and reason, distinguish fact from fiction, and question beliefs — including your own.

♡ 06

Minimize Harm, Maximize Flourishing & Love

Hover / Tap
♡ 06

Minimize Harm, Maximize Flourishing & Love

Minimize significant unnecessary harm, try to maximize flourishing, and be eager to give, receive, and reciprocate love.
