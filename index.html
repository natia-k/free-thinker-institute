Who We Are

A thoughtful community
for growth and dialogue.

We are a community of people trying to become wiser, kinder, and more grounded in how we live.

We care about reason, openness, self-improvement, honesty, and the courage to keep learning.

We believe better lives are shaped through reflection, sincere conversation, and thoughtful action.

Our aim is not perfection, but meaningful progress — individually and together.

Our Intentions
Our Intentions
Five Intentions

A shared framework for living, thinking, and growing together.

✦ 01

Devotion to Self Improvement

A sincere commitment to growing physically, intellectually, emotionally, and philosophically over time.

◌ 02

Be Transparent & Open Minded

Stay honest about who you are, remain open to different perspectives, and resist becoming rigid in your beliefs.

✳ 03

Committed to Critical Thinking

Use reflection, evidence, and careful judgment to better distinguish truth from confusion, noise, and impulse.

○ 04

Maximize Happiness While Minimizing Harm

Seek a life with freedom, wellbeing, and fulfillment while being mindful of harm to yourself and others.

♡ 05

Eager to Give and Receive Love

Build a warmer world through compassion, affection, sincerity, and meaningful human connection.

How participation works.

Who can you interact with?

Members, prospective members, teachers, speakers, and community leaders.

Where do people meet?

In person, online through chat, and through voice or video conversation.

Why participate?

To grow personally, improve professionally, and contribute to a better culture.

When can you participate?
