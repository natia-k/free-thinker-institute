The Six Intentions

A foundation for clearer growth.

These intentions shape how we think, how we work, and how we relate to others. They are not slogans. They are principles for building a more thoughtful professional life.

✦ 01

Reason, Intuition & Fairness

Tap to explore
✦ 01

Reason, Intuition & Fairness

Strong judgment comes from balancing clear reasoning, human instinct, and ethical fairness.

Practical Application: Useful in leadership, hiring, product decisions, conflict resolution, and any role where judgment affects others.

◌ 02

Self-Discovery & Self-Improvement

Tap to explore
◌ 02

Self-Discovery & Self-Improvement

Growth begins with honest self-knowledge — how you think, what drives you, and where you can develop with discipline and care.

Practical Application: Helps people choose better career paths, build confidence, improve weak areas, and grow in sustainable ways.

♡ 03

Respect, Transparency & Open-Mindedness

Tap to explore
♡ 03

Respect, Transparency & Open-Mindedness

Real progress depends on trust, honesty, humility, and the willingness to revise your views when needed.

Practical Application: Strengthens teamwork, communication, feedback culture, and collaboration across differences.

○ 04

Truth & Deep Understanding

Tap to explore
○ 04

Truth & Deep Understanding

We aim for more than quick opinions. We seek understanding that can stand up under pressure.

Practical Application: Essential for research, strategy, product discovery, systems thinking, and better long-term decisions.

✳ 05

Commitment to Critical Thinking

✳ 05

Commitment to Critical Thinking

Question assumptions, weigh evidence, and refuse to let convenience replace truth.

Practical Application: Improves problem-solving in software, business, quality assurance, operations, and leadership.

△ 06

Minimize Harm, Maximize Flourishing & Love

△ 06

Minimize Harm, Maximize Flourishing & Love

A meaningful life is not only about achievement, but about reducing harm and creating conditions where people can genuinely flourish.

Practical Application: Supports ethical leadership, user-centered design, responsible technology, and healthier teams.
