Five Intentions

Core principles that guide the community

✦ 01

Devotion to Self Improvement

A commitment to growth across physical, intellectual, emotional, and philosophical life.

◌ 02

Be Transparent & Open Minded

Stay honest about who you are and remain open to better ideas, new perspectives, and thoughtful disagreement.

✳ 03

Committed to Critical Thinking

Use evidence, reflection, and careful thought to better distinguish truth from fiction and wisdom from noise.

○ 04

Maximize Happiness While Minimizing Harm

Seek freedom, wellbeing, and fulfillment while being mindful of harm to yourself and others.

♡ 05

Eager to Give and Receive Love

Create more warmth, compassion, affection, and meaningful connection in the lives around you.

Goals

What the community hopes to build

01

Is Polite

Create a warm, compassionate, open-minded community with a richer and more meaningful social life.

02

Is Prosperous

Help people move toward enough stability and opportunity that money stops dominating wellbeing.

03

Shares Good Ideas

Create and spread ideas that increase happiness and minimize harm.

How participation works

Who can you interact with?

Members, prospective members, teachers, speakers, and organizers.

Where do we meet?

Online, through chat and calls, and in person when possible.

Why participate?

To grow personally, professionally, and socially in a thoughtful community.

When can you participate?

Through structured events and informal discussions throughout the week.

How do I join?

Start by joining the Discord and taking part in the community.

What do we discuss?

Ideas, work, growth, values, and practical ways of living better together.
