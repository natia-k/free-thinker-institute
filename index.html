Core Framework

The Five
Intentions

Five shared commitments that shape how members think, relate, work, and move through the world.

01
Devotion to Self-Improvement
A personal devotion to growth — physical, intellectual, and moral. Use reason and intuition to be the best version of yourself, seek truth, and be fair.
02
Be Transparent & Open-Minded
Be honest about who you are and what you believe — to the extent that you trust someone. Stay genuinely open to ideas that challenge you and help you grow.
03
Commitment to Critical Thinking
Develop the tools to discern fact from fiction, wisdom from folly. Think clearly so you can act rightly — and help others do the same.
04
Maximize Happiness, Minimize Harm
Attempt to maximize happiness and minimize harm for yourself and all those you can influence — and empower others who share this goal.
05
Eagerness to Give & Receive Love
An openness to give and receive love — platonic as well as romantic. Sincerity, integrity, and genuine human connection as a serious practice.
