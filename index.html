The Five Intentions
01

Self Improvement

A devotion to physical, intellectual, emotional, and philosophical growth. Strive for a constant state of evolution and treat everyone with respect.

02

Transparency

Be true to yourself. Share your authentic self and consider opinions and perspectives that differ from your own.

03

Critical Thinking

Trust and improve your own judgment. Seek objective reality through reason, evidence, and open-minded experimentation.

Our Core Goals
04

Maximize Freedom

Pursue happiness while minimizing harm to yourself and others. Freedom of thought is the engine of collective progress.

05

Prosperity

Helping members achieve financial independence so that resources serve as a tool for mission-driven work and happiness.

06

Compassionate Ideas

Spreading concepts that maximize collective well-being and empathy in a complex, globalized world.
