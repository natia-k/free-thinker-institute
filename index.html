Five Intentions

Core principles that shape the community

✦ 01

Devotion to Self Improvement

A commitment to growth across physical, intellectual, emotional, and philosophical life.

◌ 02

Be Transparent & Open Minded

Stay honest about who you are and remain open to new perspectives, ideas, and better reasoning.

✳ 03

Committed to Critical Thinking

Use evidence, thoughtfulness, and reflection to better distinguish truth from fiction.

○ 04

Maximize Happiness While Minimizing Harm

Seek freedom and wellbeing while reducing unnecessary harm to yourself and others.

♡ 05

Eager to Give and Receive Love

Build a warmer world through care, affection, sincerity, and meaningful human connection.

How participation works

Who can you interact with?

Members, prospective members, teachers, speakers, and organizers.

Why participate?

To grow personally, professionally, and socially in a thoughtful community.

When can you participate?

Through structured events and informal conversations throughout the week.

How do I join?
