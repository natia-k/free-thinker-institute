Six Intentions

A shared framework for
living and growing.

Six intentions shape how we think, relate, grow, seek truth, and build careers with more clarity, depth, and care.

✦01

Reason, Intuition & Fairness

Hover / Tap
✦01

Reason, Intuition & Fairness

Strong judgment comes from balancing clear reasoning, human instinct, and ethical fairness. This intention helps people think well without becoming cold, and care deeply without losing clarity.

Practical Application: Useful in leadership, hiring, product decisions, conflict resolution, and any role where good judgment affects other people.

◌02

Self-Discovery & Self-Improvement

Hover / Tap
◌02

Self-Discovery & Self-Improvement

Growth begins with honest self-knowledge. This intention helps people understand how they think, what drives them, and where they can develop with discipline and care.

Practical Application: Helps people choose better career paths, build confidence, improve weak areas, and grow in ways that are sustainable rather than performative.

♡03

Respect, Transparency & Open-Mindedness

Hover / Tap
♡03

Respect, Transparency & Open-Mindedness

Real progress depends on trust. This intention encourages respectful dialogue, intellectual humility, and the courage to be honest about what you know, what you do not know, and what may need revision.

Practical Application: Strengthens teamwork, communication, feedback culture, and the ability to collaborate well across differences.

○04

Truth & Deep Understanding

Hover / Tap
○04

Truth & Deep Understanding

We aim for more than quick opinions or surface-level certainty. This intention is about listening deeply, examining reality carefully, and seeking understanding that can stand up under pressure.

Practical Application: Essential for research, strategy, product discovery, systems thinking, and making better long-term decisions.

✳05

Commitment to Critical Thinking

Hover / Tap
✳05

Commitment to Critical Thinking

Critical thinking means questioning assumptions, weighing evidence, and refusing to let convenience replace truth. It builds intellectual independence in a noisy world.

Practical Application: Improves problem-solving in software, business, quality assurance, operations, and any field where clear thinking matters.

△06

Minimize Harm, Maximize Flourishing & Love

Hover / Tap
△06

Minimize Harm, Maximize Flourishing & Love

A meaningful life is not only about achievement. This intention asks how our choices affect others, how we reduce unnecessary harm, and how we create conditions in which people can genuinely flourish.

Practical Application: Supports ethical leadership, user-centered design, responsible technology, healthier teams, and more human ways of working.
